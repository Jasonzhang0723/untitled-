My Project Statment

Overall, this is a project that speaks on pragmatism in modern Chinese society. By employing a common cultural symbol(s) that exists in Chinese culture and subverting the same ideology on the deity by asking them to “bless themselves.” By purchasing fifty (or more) statues of the same deity and placing them in physically vulnerable areas around the city of Chicago for X amount of time(days) and seeing which ones were capable of “blessing itself” and therefore is deemed “the real Buddha.” Second, after Y amount of the “true Buddha” is discovered, it will be sold or auctioned for Z amount of dollars; in reference to value, worth, and desire. Prior to the process of “testing” each Buddha, a website and an Instagram page will be created to promote and document the process of each statue.
